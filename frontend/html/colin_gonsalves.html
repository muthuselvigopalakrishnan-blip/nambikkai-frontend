Colin Gonsalves

Advancing social justice through law, rights, and constitutional values.

Studies / Education

• Law degree from India.
• Further legal studies and training in human rights law.

Type of Lawyer

A senior advocate and human-rights lawyer specialising in constitutional law, labour rights, tribal rights, environmental justice, and public interest litigation.

Years of Experience

He has over four decades of experience in legal practice and human-rights advocacy. He was designated as a Senior Advocate by the Supreme Court of India.

Summary

Colin Gonsalves is a prominent Indian senior advocate and one of the country’s leading public interest and human-rights lawyers. Based in New Delhi, he is the founder of the Human Rights Law Network (HRLN), which provides legal support to marginalized and vulnerable communities across India. His work focuses on access to justice for workers, women, children, tribal communities, and victims of social and economic injustice. Widely respected in India and internationally, he is known for his strong commitment to constitutional rights, equality, and social justice.
