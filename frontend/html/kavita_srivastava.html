Kavita Srivastava

Advocating human dignity, civil liberties, and social justice.

Studies / Education

• Law degree from India (Faculty of Law, University of Delhi).
• Trained and educated in law and human-rights advocacy.

Type of Lawyer

A human-rights lawyer and civil liberties activist specialising in constitutional rights, civil liberties, women’s rights, and protection of marginalized communities.

Years of Experience

She has over three decades of experience working in the field of law, civil liberties, and human rights advocacy.

Summary

Kavita Srivastava is a well-known Indian human-rights lawyer and activist based in India. She is widely recognised for her work on civil liberties, constitutional rights, and justice for vulnerable and marginalised communities. She has served as the President of the People’s Union for Civil Liberties (PUCL) and has been actively involved in documenting human rights violations, supporting victims of violence, and strengthening democratic freedoms. Through her legal work and activism, she continues to play a significant role in promoting justice, equality, and the rule of law in India.
