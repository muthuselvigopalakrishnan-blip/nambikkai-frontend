Anand Grover

Advancing justice through human rights, public health, and constitutional law.

Studies / Education

• Law degree from the University of Mumbai.
• Trained as an advocate in India with a focus on public interest law.

Type of Lawyer

A senior advocate and human-rights lawyer specialising in public interest litigation, constitutional law, health rights, and social justice.

Years of Experience

He has over four decades of experience in legal practice and human-rights advocacy. He was designated as a Senior Advocate by the Supreme Court of India.

Summary

Anand Grover is a renowned Indian senior advocate and a leading human-rights lawyer based in New Delhi. He is the co-founder of the Lawyers Collective, an organisation known for its work on public interest litigation, health rights, and access to justice. He has contributed significantly to legal reforms related to HIV/AIDS, public health, women’s rights, and civil liberties. Widely respected in India and internationally, he is recognised for his strong commitment to constitutional values, equality, and the protection of human rights.
