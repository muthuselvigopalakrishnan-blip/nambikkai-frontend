AASARA Foundation

Empowering communities through compassion, education, and sustainable change.

Who We Are

AASARA Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting the lives of underprivileged and marginalized communities across India. Established in [2002] by [AASARA], the foundation was born out of a vision to create a more compassionate and equal society where every person can access education, healthcare, and dignity.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to bring hope (“Aasara” means support or shelter) to those in need by enabling self-reliance and promoting equality. We envision a world where every individual, regardless of background, has the opportunity to thrive through access to education, wellness, and empowerment.

Core Focus Areas

Education & Child Welfare: Providing learning resources, scholarships, and mentorship to children from low-income families.

Healthcare & Wellness: Conducting medical camps, health awareness drives, and nutrition programs to support families in need.

Women Empowerment: Promoting self-help groups, vocational training, and financial literacy for women to achieve independence.

Community Development: Supporting local initiatives, providing basic resources, and encouraging social inclusion.
