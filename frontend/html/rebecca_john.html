Rebecca John

Defending justice through integrity, courage, and the rule of law.

Studies / Education

• Law degree from the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi.
• Enrolled as an Advocate with the Bar Council of Delhi.

Type of Lawyer

A senior criminal lawyer specialising in criminal defence, constitutional law, and cases related to human rights and personal liberty.

Years of Experience

She has been practising law for over three decades and was designated as a Senior Advocate by the Delhi High Court in 2013.

Summary

Rebecca John is a prominent Indian criminal lawyer based in New Delhi. She is known for handling complex and high-profile criminal cases with a strong commitment to due process and fair trial rights. In 2013, she became the first woman to be designated as a Senior Advocate on the criminal side by the Delhi High Court. Over her long legal career, she has represented both accused persons and victims in significant criminal trials, earning respect for her legal expertise, independence, and dedication to justice.
