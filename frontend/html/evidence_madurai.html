Evidence - Madurai

“From Madurai’s Heart, Empowering Every Future.”

Mission & Vision:

Mission: To offer “support” or “shelter” (as the name AASARA implies) to those in need by enabling self-reliance, promoting education, healthcare, empowerment and community development. Vision: A society where every individual, no matter their background, has the opportunity to thrive through access to learning, wellness, and empowerment.

Core Focus Areas:

Education & Child Welfare – Providing learning resources, scholarships, mentorship to children from low-income families.
Healthcare & Wellness – Running health-awareness drives, providing basic medical support and nutrition programmes.
Women Empowerment – Promoting self-help groups, vocational training, financial literacy for women to gain independence.
Community Development – Supporting local initiatives, providing basic resources, and promoting social inclusion.
