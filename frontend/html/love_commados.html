Love Commados

“From Madurai’s Heart, Empowering Every Future.”

Who They Are

Love Commandos is a voluntary non-profit organisation in India founded in July 2010. It was established to provide protection, legal aid and safe shelter to couples who face harassment, ostracism or threats from their families or communities for marrying by choice, especially across caste, religion or class lines.

Mission & Vision

Their mission is to uphold the right to love, marriage and personal choice without fear of violence or discrimination — going beyond tradition and enabling couples to claim safety, dignity and freedom. They envision an India where love — regardless of caste or creed — is met with support rather than suppression.

Core Focus Areas

Providing a 24-hour helpline and network of volunteers across India to respond to distress calls from couples. Offering legal counselling, shelter in safe houses and assistance in registration of marriages for couples under threat. Advocating against honour killings, discriminatory norms and violence that stem from family or community opposition to love marriages.

Impact & Challenges

Since its inception, Love Commandos has helped thousands of couples across India navigate threats and gain protection. Delhi Police arrested the founder on allegations of extortion, illegal confinement and intimidation of couples seeking refuge.

Looking Ahead

Love Commandos continues to highlight the plight of couples forced into secrecy or danger because of social constraints, and seeks to strengthen its network of volunteers, improve transparency and challenge the stigma associated with love marriages in conservative societies.
