Indira Jaising

Championing equality, constitutional values, and women’s rights.

Studies / Education

• Law degree from the University of Mumbai.
• Studied law and trained as an advocate in India.

Type of Lawyer

A senior advocate and constitutional lawyer specialising in women’s rights, human rights, public interest litigation, and constitutional law.

Years of Experience

She has over four decades of experience in legal practice and public advocacy. She was designated as a Senior Advocate by the Supreme Court of India.

Summary

Indira Jaising is one of India’s most respected senior advocates and a leading voice in constitutional and human rights law. Based in New Delhi, she has played a key role in advancing women’s rights, gender equality, and access to justice through litigation and law reform. She is the founder of the Lawyers Collective, an organisation that provides legal support and advocacy on issues such as gender justice, health rights, and civil liberties. Widely recognised in India and internationally, she is known for her strong commitment to the Constitution, social justice, and the rule of law.
