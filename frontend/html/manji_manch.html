Manjhi Manch

"Where Connections Create Change."

Who We Are

Manjhi Manch is an organisation based in India that blends community service with matrimonial matchmaking. According to its website, it describes itself as “India’s pioneer online matrimonial service provider … with NGO work as well”.

Our Mission & Vision

Their mission centres on the belief that “each and everyone is made for someone special”, and they aim to provide a platform for life-partner matching across religions and communities, while also engaging in social service. They envision a society where meaningful relationships and community uplift go hand-in-hand.

Core Focus Areas

Matchmaking Services:Facilitating connections between prospective brides and grooms from a wide background of religions and communities. Community & NGO Work: Although the specific details are less publicly documented, Manjhi Manch also positions itself as doing NGO work alongside its matchmaking function. Support-Services: Mentioning “we are dedicated to do our work for you … 10+ years of professional experience” suggests they provide guidance and support for users of their service.

Impact & Milestones

Over a decade of operation in the matchmaking field. A large database of prospective bride and groom profiles across communities. Their dual identity as service provider + social organisation gives them a unique niche, though concrete social-impact metrics are not prominently available on the public site.

Looking Ahead

Manjhi Manch plans to continue strengthening its matchmaking platform while deepening its social service side. Their future focus could include more transparent reporting of their NGO work, expanding outreach across rural and marginalised communities, and combining relationship support with empowerment initiatives
