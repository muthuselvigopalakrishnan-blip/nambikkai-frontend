Type of Lawyer

Years of Experience

She has been practising since 1989 — over 30 years of advocacy. She is noted as having 34+ years in recent sources.

Summary

Vrinda Grover-ji is a leading Indian advocate and human-rights lawyer based in New Delhi. She combines deep legal expertise in criminal and constitutional law with a long track-record working on gender-based violence, communal violence, state impunity and human rights issues. Her academic credentials include a law degree in India and an LL.M. from the USA. With decades of practice and several landmark cases to her credit, she is widely recognised in India and internationally for her commitment to justice and rights.
